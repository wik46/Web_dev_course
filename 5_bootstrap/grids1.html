Learning Grids

How to use grids.

The grid system is split into 12 little columns.

Breakpoints are the screen size at which the page changes.

Class: col-sm-4 means we want a column that is 4 of the mini-columns wide. And the sm means that the breakpoint will occur at a small distance. If you use lg, then the columns will stack earlier when the screen is smaller.
